Thailand

Thailand! A country that has a rich and diverse culture. Renowned for it's beautiful beaches, picturesque landscapes and delicious food. Boasting a tropical climate, meaning it is warm and sunny most of the year round. You can be sure to enjoy the sunshine whilst rejoicing in everything Thailand has to offer.

Offering a vast array of experiences, Thailand is undoubtedly the place to be! From swimming with elephants, participating in a thai cooking classes, admiring unique temples to experiencing the vivid nightlife. To top all this off, Thailand is super cheap! Meaning you can enjoy all different types of accomodation to suit your budget.

Japan

If you choose to visit Japan, it will be a moment in time you shall never forget! From the bustling, modern metropolis of Tokyo to the enchanting towns like Kyoto, Nara and Takayama to name a few. A culture shock of a different kind that will be sure to blow your mind!

There is so much that Japan has to offer... where to start? Ramen, robots, ridiculously beautiful sights. Witness the super-advanced technology such as the unrivaled bullet trains or immerse yourself in the anime culture. Nature lover? Japan also has you covered! Admire the breathtaking beauty of Hanami season or visit the perfect volcanic cone of Mt.Fuji.

Indonesia

Luscious green rice fields, volcanic scenary and a plethora of mystical islands sound like your thing? Then Indonesia should be on your list. Feel welcomed by the kind locals, indulge in the diverse culture, whilst being wowed by the gorgeous natures, such as tropical rainforests, tranquil beaches and mountains.

Journey across different islands, find yourself that perfect souvenir at one of the many markets, witness the wonders under the crystal clear waters or just sit back and relax on one of the many white, pink or black sand beaches. Accomodation wise, Indonesia has it all. Find yourself nestled in a beautiful beach hut or a luxurious resort, all without breaking the bank.
